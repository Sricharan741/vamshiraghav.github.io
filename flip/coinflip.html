To Win The Game ?: Get A Minimum Score Of 100 Or Score More Than Computer Score WithIn 20 Trials To Complete The Game.
Game Terms:
  • Computer Score = Total Bet Won By Computer ;
  • Your Score = Total Bet Won By Player ;
  • Tosses = Number Of Coins To Toss At Once;
  • 1 Trial=Tossing 'n' Coins At Once.
Game Scoring Rules:
  1. When Your Score Is Zero , You Are Provided With A Bet Upto 25 RS/-.
  2. When Your Score Is Not Zero , You Can Bet Upto Your Score Value.
  3. Enter The Bet And Heads Count Based On The Constraints Mentioned Dynamically.
  4. Each Time You Win , Bet Will Be Added To Your Score And Bet Is Subtracted From Computer Score
  5. Each Time You Lose , Bet Will Be Subtracted from Your Score And Bet Is Added To The Computer Score
  6. Player Can Change The Bet And No. Of Heads At Every Trial.
